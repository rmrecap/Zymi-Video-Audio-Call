# ZYMI Community Guidelines

**DRAFT — NOT LEGAL ADVICE. Must be reviewed by a qualified attorney before publication.**

**Last Updated: June 1, 2026**

---

## Our Philosophy

ZYMI is built for real, private conversations. We believe in giving people space to communicate freely — but that freedom comes with responsibility. These guidelines exist to keep ZYMI a safe, respectful place for everyone.

We're firm on safety. We're friendly by default. Let's keep it that way.

---

## 1. Be Respectful

Treat others the way you want to be treated. Disagreement is fine. Harassment is not.

- **Do**: Debate ideas, share opinions, express yourself.
- **Don't**: Attack individuals, insult groups, or engage in personal vendettas.

---

## 2. No Harassment

Harassment of any kind is not tolerated. This includes:
- Targeted abuse or bullying.
- Sending unwanted sexual content or advances.
- Repeatedly contacting a user who has asked you to stop.
- Creating groups or channels for the purpose of harassing others.
- Doxxing (sharing another person's private information without consent).

---

## 3. No Hate Speech

ZYMI is not a platform for hate. We prohibit content that promotes or incites hatred against individuals or groups based on:
- Race, ethnicity, or national origin.
- Religion or creed.
- Gender, gender identity, or sexual orientation.
- Disability or medical condition.
- Age or any other protected characteristic.

---

## 4. No Illegal Content

Do not use ZYMI to share, promote, or engage in illegal activity. This includes:
- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) — zero tolerance. We report all CSAM to authorities.
- Illegal drug sales or promotion.
- Weapons trafficking.
- Fraud, scams, or phishing.
- Copyright-infringing material.

---

## 5. No Spam

Don't spam. This includes:
- Unsolicited commercial messages or advertising.
- Mass messaging or broadcast-style communication.
- Posting the same message repeatedly across chats.
- Links to malware, phishing sites, or deceptive content.
- Cryptocurrency scams or "get rich quick" schemes.

---

## 6. No Impersonation

Do not impersonate:
- ZYMI administrators or staff.
- Other ZYMI users.
- Public figures, brands, or organizations.

Parody accounts are allowed if clearly labeled as such. Impersonation for deception is not.

---

## 7. Protecting Privacy

Respect other people's privacy.
- Do not share private information about others without their consent (this includes real names, addresses, phone numbers, private messages, photos).
- Do not record or share screenshots of private conversations without all participants' consent.
- Do not attempt to access another user's account or messages.

---

## 8. Explicit Content

ZYMI is a general-purpose communication platform. While we don't ban all mature content, we prohibit:
- Pornography or sexually explicit media.
- Graphic violence or gore.
- Content depicting self-harm or suicide.

Private, consensual conversations between adults are generally not moderated — but if someone reports content as unwanted harassment, we may take action.

---

## 9. Reporting Violations

If you see something that violates these guidelines:

### In-App Report
1. Open the chat or user profile.
2. Tap the menu (...) and select **Report**.
3. Choose the reason and submit.

### Email Report
Send details to the admin team at: [admin email — to be inserted]

Please include:
- The username(s) involved.
- Screenshots or relevant evidence.
- A brief description of the violation.

Our response SLAs:
- **Critical** (illegal content, CSAM, immediate threat): Within 24 hours.
- **Standard** (harassment, spam, impersonation): Within 7 days.

---

## 10. Consequences

We use a progressive enforcement model:

| Violation | First Offense | Second Offense | Severe / Repeated |
|-----------|---------------|----------------|-------------------|
| Minor (spam, mild disrespect) | Warning | 7-day temporary ban | Permanent ban |
| Moderate (harassment, impersonation) | 7-day temporary ban | 30-day temporary ban | Permanent ban |
| Severe (hate speech, threats, illegal content) | Permanent ban | — | Account deletion |
| CSAM / Illegal activity | Immediate account deletion + report to authorities | — | — |

**Permanent ban** means your account is disabled and you may not re-register. Attempting to create a new account to evade a ban will result in immediate deletion of the new account.

---

## 11. Appeals Process

If you believe a moderation action was taken in error, you can appeal:

1. **Email** the admin team at [admin email — to be inserted] with the subject line: **"Appeal — [your username]"**
2. Include:
   - Your username.
   - The action taken (warning, ban duration).
   - Why you believe the action was incorrect.
   - Any supporting evidence.

Appeals are reviewed within 7 days. You will receive a response via email. Decisions on appeal are final.

---

## 12. Reporting False Violations

Filing a false or malicious report to get another user in trouble is itself a violation of these guidelines. Users who abuse the reporting system may face consequences up to and including account deletion.

---

## 13. Final Notes

These guidelines apply to all areas of ZYMI: private chats, group chats, voice/video calls, profile information, usernames, and status messages.

ZYMI administrators have the final say in interpreting and enforcing these guidelines. We reserve the right to take any action we deem necessary to keep the community safe.
